About Us

LT Fitness & Performance is a community-focused organization dedicated to fostering self-esteem, confidence, and healthy habits in both youth and adults. Founded in 2021 by Latoya, who grew up overcoming asthma through fitness, our programs emphasize inclusivity and empowerment. From our Self-Love Running Camp to personalized Personal Training sessions, we aim to instill a love for physical activity while nurturing positive self-image and mindset. Through group fitness training and community events, we provide safe spaces for learning about health and wellness. Join us to embark on a journey of self-discovery, fitness, and belonging in a supportive environment.
