Circuit Training

Circuit Training

Our goal is to empower youth and adults by helping them increase self-esteem, confidence, and self love through running. We offer the camp to children 3 to 10 years old of all fitness levels. The benefit of starting kids at an early age is not just physical but for social and emotionally health. We combine core principles with running drills to create a curriculum that pairs physical health with a self-love mindset. Kids are given a swag bag in week one with T-shirt that is worn the whole camp. Water bottle, jump rope.

Our Core Principles:

  • Teamwork – we can do together what we cannot do alone.
  • Positive Thoughts – we are what we think.
  • Aggressive goal – believing anything is possible.
