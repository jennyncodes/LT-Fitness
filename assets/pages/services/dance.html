Dance

Get ready to move, groove, and sweat it out in our Dance Fitness class at LT!

Join us for our Dance Fitness class and experience the joy of movement like never before!

Led by our talented and energetic instructors, our Dance Fitness class offers a fun and effective way to burn calories, improve coordination, and unleash your inner dancer.

What to Expect:

  • Fun and engaging routines that boost mood and reduce stress.
  • Easy-to-follow choreography suitable for beginners and experienced dancers alike.
  • Cardiovascular exercises to elevate your heart rate and burn calories.
  • Dynamic movements that target major muscle groups and improve overall fitness.
  • A supportive and non-judgmental atmosphere where you can let loose and have fun.
